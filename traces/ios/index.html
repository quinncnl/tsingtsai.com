Traces for iOS

Remember where you've been

Download on the App Store Requires iOS 8.0+, certain features require iOS 9.0, adapted for both iPhone and iPad

Features

Apple Map Enhanced for Ourdoor Activity

  • Support all four orientations.
  • Zoom with single finger - tap and drag.

Record Your Position

  • Recording your walking, running, cycling tracks, or bus and train tracks.
  • Files are saved to iCloud Drive, and automatically synchronized to all of your iOS devices and Mac.
  • File format is standard GPX. You can download these files from iCloud Drive and use them for other purposes.
  • Reduced GPX file size without compromizing on accuracy.
  • A little longer battery life.

View Your Saved Tracks

  • Show different types of tracks in different colors.
  • Draw all your GPX files on a single map, so you know where you've been. Good for exploring cities and traveling around the world.
  • You can put your existing GPX files to iCloud Drive and they will be on your map.
  • Partial offline map and 3D buildings thanks to Apple Maps.
