Traces for iOS

Features

Recording Your Position

  • Recording your walking, running, cycling tracks, or bus and train tracks.
  • Files are saved to iCloud Drive, and can be syncrhonized to all of your iOS devices and Mac.
  • File format is standard GPX, so you can use it for other purposes.
  • Reduced GPX file size without compromizing on accuracy.
  • A little longer battery life.

Viewing Your Saved Tracks

  • Show different types of tracks in different colors.
  • Put all your GPX files on a single map, so you know where you've been. Good for exploring cities and traveling around the world.
  • You can put your existing GPX files to iCloud Drive and they will be on your map.
  • Since it uses Apple Maps, you enjoy all the benefits such as offline maps and 3D buildings.
